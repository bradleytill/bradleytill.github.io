Christmas 2002

    Our very first Christmas CD mix! The idea came about during holiday parties where people would explain that they hate Christmas music, but loved what we were playing. We thought it would be fun to share all the holiday songs we loved. The front cover is from a "Our Lady of Guadalupe" candle. The back page art is a photo montage using the cover of a record of Norwegian folk songs (guy with a horse and guitar) and figurines of the abominable snowman and a monkey wearing a fez. Because nothing says Christmas like that combination of things. CD art is a photo of Santa from a chldren's Christmas record.
